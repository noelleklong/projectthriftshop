PROJECT THRIFT SHOP
Not just a thrift store — Project Thrift Shop ECT and Project Loose Change are a movement of hope, love, and community.
Our Mission We believe a store can be more than a place to shop. It can be a safe haven, a hub of opportunity, and a space where families, seniors, youth, and even pets find the support they need to thrive. Our mission is to create affordable access to essentials while building programs that uplift people and protect the animals we love. From clothing, furniture, and household goods at low cost, to food support, job training, senior care, mental wellness, and free pet microchipping, every initiative is designed to restore dignity and build stronger communities. And of course — we want you to come shop with us! Every purchase keeps our doors open, helps us pay the bills (yes, even that $3,000 rent), and allows us to keep building this legacy of love and service. When you shop here, you're not just finding treasures — you're fueling transformation. About Us For over 22 years, Project Loose Change Inc. has served children, seniors, families, veterans, and individuals in crisis through toy drives, school supply giveaways, food distributions, and emergency relief.

Our partner, Project Thrift Shop ECT, takes those efforts further by creating a sustainable cycle of giving. The shop funds outreach programs, hosts events like Miracle Mondays and Crazy Wednesdays, and now provides resources like free pet microchipping — because people and animals both deserve care and protection.

Together, we are more than a business. We are a community hub, a faith-driven mission, and a family of helpers — and with your support, we'll keep growing and giving for years to come.
